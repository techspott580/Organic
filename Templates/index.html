What is Organic Farming?

Organic farming is an agricultural method that focuses on growing crops in harmony with nature. It relies on natural fertilizers like compost, biological pest control, and crop rotation to maintain soil health and reduce the environmental impact.

Benefits of Organic Farming:

Promotes biodiversity

Improves soil structure

Reduces chemical pollution

Enhances food safety and quality
